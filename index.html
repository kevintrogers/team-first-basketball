About

Coach Jeff Davis has been coaching basketball since 1999 at the junior high, high school, and college levels. He takes pride in his commitment to understanding indivual players strengths and weaknesses so that they can build opon their foundation and help players evolve into more well-rounded athletes.

Past experience includes coaching opportunities at Steele Canyon High School, Barrett High School and Saddleback Junior College. Coach Davis provided scouting assistance at these locations as well as helping players to build their fundamentals and overall understanding of the game.

Coach Davis is a dedicated teacher devoted to helping athletes become the best young men and women that they can become.
